# ResearchFin

ResearchFin is a web-based research budget planning and funding discovery platform designed to help students and academic researchers manage project expenses, explore funding opportunities, and optimise research costs efficiently.

The platform combines budgeting tools, funding guidance, cost-optimisation strategies, and academic case studies into a single unified dashboard experience.

---

## Overview

Research projects often fail due to poor financial planning, lack of awareness about available funding sources, and inefficient resource management.

ResearchFin aims to solve this problem by providing:

- Research budget planning tools
- Funding source discovery
- Expense optimisation strategies
- Financial self-assessment
- Research case studies
- Open-source tool recommendations

---

## Features

### Budget Planner
Create and manage research budgets with estimated project costs and optimisation suggestions.

### Funding Finder
Explore institutional, government, industry, and competition-based funding opportunities for academic projects.

### Financial Assessment
Interactive assessment module for evaluating financial constraints and research feasibility.

### Case Study Explorer
Browse real-world research project case studies with cost-reduction strategies and outcomes.

### Research Strategy Framework
Structured financial planning strategies for improving research efficiency and reducing project costs.

### Open-Source Tool Recommendations
Discover free and open-source alternatives to expensive research software and tools.
